Privacy Policy

Last updated July 16, 2026.

Xtreme Mobile Detailing ("we," "us," "our") operates this website. This page explains what information we collect, why, and the choices you have. We keep it in plain English — if anything is unclear, email us at xtrememobiledetailing7@gmail.com.

Information we collect

When you use our quote request form, we collect what you enter: your name, phone number, vehicle make & model, the service you're interested in, and any notes you add. We use this only to respond to your request and schedule your appointment.

Like most websites, we also automatically collect some technical information when you visit — your IP address, browser and device type, and which pages you view. If Google Analytics is enabled on this site (see below), that information is collected in more detail through cookies to help us understand site traffic.

Cookies & Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use this site — which pages are popular, how people find us, and where to improve. Google Analytics uses cookies (small files stored in your browser) to do this. We do not currently use Meta Pixel, Google Ads conversion tracking, remarketing, or any other advertising pixel — if that changes, we'll update this policy and add an opt-out option here.

You can prevent analytics cookies by adjusting your browser's cookie settings, or by installing Google's Analytics opt-out browser add-on. Google's own use of data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Other services we use

Our quote form is delivered using Web3Forms, a third-party form-delivery service — the information you submit is sent through their system to our inbox. Our web fonts are served by Fontshare, which loads font files but does not set tracking cookies.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with the service providers above as needed to run this website and respond to your requests.

Your choices

To request that we delete information you've submitted through our contact form, email us at xtrememobiledetailing7@gmail.com and we'll take care of it. See the cookies section above for how to opt out of analytics.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
