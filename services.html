Ceramic Coating

The greatest protection you can give your paint

A ceramic coating is a chemical substance that, once applied correctly, offers extreme hydrophobicity and protection against harmful UV rays — giving the paint a higher gloss and making it 10x easier to clean.

We offer three coatings that last 1–2 years, 3–4 years, or 5–6 years. The vehicle needs at least 12–24 hours away from water to cure, and 2–3 days without soaps or other chemicals touching it to fully harden.

Extreme hydrophobicity

Water and grime bead off instead of sticking.

UV & water-spot protection

Shields against sun damage and chemical etching.

Enhanced gloss

Super slick finish that's easy to wash and wipe clean.

Every customer who books ceramic coating with us gets a free starter kit to maintain the coating, plus a free first follow-up wash. We'll walk you through how to wash the vehicle correctly to protect the coating's longevity — with an optional maintenance plan available.
